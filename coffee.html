My Love of Coffee

My love for coffee began like most - keeping me awake during my studies!

But my enjoyment of coffee didn't end there. I began my experience with coffee brewing espresso. Fortunately, I have always worked with independent coffee companies which kept me open to a range of different beans from a range of locations and farmers, feeding my curiosity for getting more flavour out of the beans I had been working with and opening up the world of other brewing methods. Coming from an english household I began with a cafeti√®re (French press) and soon purchased my own V60, Moka Pot, Kalita and Aeropress. And so began my speciality coffee journey.

Since then I have had barista, brewing and sensory training with Origin Coffee, Allpress Espresso and SCA (Specialist Coffee Association), and over 5 years of onsite barista experience.

Although I enjoy putting a lot of love and experience into my coffee, my core commitment is towards the customer experience; improving people's day, listening to them, educating them and bettering their daily experience through the coffee and hospitality.

Currently, I am based in Cambridge, brewing coffee at WeWork using Change Please Coffee.
