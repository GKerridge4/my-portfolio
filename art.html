My Art, Poetry & Free Thinking

I do not have formal training with a paintbrush, nor academic knowledge of the written word; what I do have is an open mind for creativity and experiences I strive to express through a multitude of creative means.

I consider myself a professional in auditory expression due to my years of education, study and experience in music and because of this, I appreciate the myriad of trials and lessons that come with any art form. This is why I do not currently call myself a professional with more physical or literate art forms. This does not mean though, that I do not believe in my other forms of expression and give them equal meaning within my self-expression.

To physically represent one's feelings on a canvas, on the page or through the screen is a necessary and fundamental act that any creative will face along their journey. For a long time, I have lacked confidence in these forms of expression as I have feared my lack of professionalism in comparison to my music.

In this space, you will find common threads across all of my artistic outlets.
