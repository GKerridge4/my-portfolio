Primitive Talks

A project started during university with a good friend and long time collaborator, Fabian.
Initial born as 'Aurora', a live performance for Fabian's degree dissertation, this project brought together our love for experimental electronica and drum and bass.

Although we don't have any spotify releases under our belt yet, you can check out the Aurora project below and watch Fabian and I perform together with Caswell and Paint Factory.
